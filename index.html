About Synaptics

Synaptics 2K26 is the premier national-level technical symposium organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at JNTUH University College of Engineering Sultanpur (UCES). Designed as a nexus for innovation, it challenges the brightest minds to bridge the gap between theoretical hardware design and the future of intelligent systems.

The Department of ECE at JNTUH UCES, established with a vision to produce globally competent engineers, boasts state-of-the-art laboratory facilities including VLSI design, Embedded Systems, and Microwave Communications. Our academic rigor is matched by a commitment to research excellence, fostering an environment where students push the boundaries of signal processing, robotics, and neural engineering within the prestigious JNTU Hyderabad ecosystem.
