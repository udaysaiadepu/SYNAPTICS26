About Synaptics

Synaptics 2K26 is the premier national-level technical symposium of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at JNTUH UCES. Focusing on the convergence of neural computation and hardware engineering, we provide a platform for students to showcase expertise in VLSI, Embedded Systems, and AI-driven signal processing. Inspired by the prestigious JNTU Hyderabad legacy, our Sultanpur campus fosters innovation through rigorous competition and collaborative research.
