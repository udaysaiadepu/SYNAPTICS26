About Synaptics

Synaptics 2K26 is the premier national-level technical symposium hosted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at JNTUH University College of Engineering Sultanpur (UCES). Focusing on the convergence of neural networks and modern hardware engineering, we provide a high-caliber platform for students to demonstrate proficiency in VLSI, Embedded Systems, and Signal Processing. By bridging the gap between theoretical academia and industry-standard innovation, Synaptics fosters the next generation of technical leaders within the prestigious JNTU Hyderabad ecosystem.
