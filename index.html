About Synaptics

Synaptics 2K26 is the premier national-level technical symposium of the Department of ECE at JNTUH UCES. It serves as a convergence point for future engineers to explore VLSI, Embedded Systems, and Neural Networks. Organized by the dedicated students of ECE, this fest pushes the boundaries of innovation and technical excellence within the JNTU Hyderabad ecosystem.

Core Nodes

Neural Interface

Advanced workshop on BCI (Brain-Computer Interface) and signal processing at a university-research level.

Logic Flux

A competitive circuit design arena focusing on high-speed hardware optimization.
